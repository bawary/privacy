# Privacy Policy – Watch2o
_Last updated: March 2026_

Watch2o is an Apple Watch application designed for simple and fast water‑intake tracking.  
Your privacy is important. This app is built to respect your data and does not collect or share any personal information.

## Information We Collect
Watch2o does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.  
No user identifiers, analytics, tracking tools, or personal information are used by the app.

## Health Data (if applicable)
If you choose to enable HealthKit to store water‑intake data:
- Data is stored only on your device.
- Data is never sent to external servers.
- Watch2o does not access any other HealthKit data types.

## Third‑Party Sharing
Watch2o does **not** share any data with third parties.

## Children’s Privacy
Watch2o does not target or collect data from children.

Any changes will be updated on this page.
